Genrython

A potential universal and simplified omics ontology analysis tool. (+ Network propagation analysis)

Motivation

During my first period as a bioinformatician trainee, I have found that many of the tools used in bioinformatics are web-based, have an use interface or are simply private. As a way to learn more about computer science as a biologist I am commited to develop small tools that I have need in a certain moment with the best informatic praxis (with a bit of supervision from @angrykoala).
